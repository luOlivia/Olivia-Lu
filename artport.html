Drowning

On ocean pollution and oil spills

This piece personifies the beauty of the ocean. At the surface level, many people don’t see the pollution that is lying underneath the vastness of the water or the glistening tides. All they see are their little “bubbles” of reality, shown in the circles surrounding the girl. Instead of the millions of plastic bottles or dangerous fish nets, they see the delicate fish or the swirling waves. But this beauty has been slowly encroached upon by human-created pollution. All the plastic, oil, and trash is beginning to engulf the girl and destroy the ocean habitats we’ve come to know and love. With its subtle details and thoughtful message, this drawing presents a pressing issue that needs to be addressed to create a healthy environment.

  • Medium: Ink on Paper
  • Size: 8.5 in x 11 in
  • Date: 2017
